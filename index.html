--- layout: page author_profile: true --- Işık İlber Sırmatel received the B.Sc. degrees in mechanical and control engineering from Istanbul Technical University, Turkey, and the M.Sc. degree in mechanical engineering from Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich (ETH Zurich), Switzerland, in 2010, 2012, and 2014, respectively. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering at the Urban Transport Systems Laboratory (LUTS), Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPF Lausanne), Switzerland. His research interests include automatic control, optimization, and model predictive control, with applications to control of transportation systems.
